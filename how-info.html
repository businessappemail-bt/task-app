Tap Post, choose the task type, answer the questions, add the task address and date needed, then review and post.

Workers review your task and send quote offers. You can compare the worker, rating, message, and quote amount before accepting.

You pay after accepting a worker quote. If you saved a default payment method, the payment page can pull those saved display details.

The arrival code confirms the worker arrived for the job. Only give the code to the assigned worker once they are actually there.

Tasks & Scheduling
Can I edit or cancel a task? ›
You can edit open tasks before they are completed. Cancel options depend on the task status and whether a worker has already been accepted. Please note that there is a cancellation policy that is strictly enforced to protect workers.
what is your cancellation policy? ›
If no worker is assigned you may cancel for free. If a worker is assigned, a cancellation fee may apply depending on how close the cancellation is to the scheduled task time.

Cancel 7+ days or more - no fee.

Cancel 3-7 days before the scheduled task - 10% cancellation fee.

Cancel 24-72 hours before the scheduled task - 20% cancellation fee.

Same day cancellations - 30% cancellation fee.

A portion of the cancellation will be paid to the worker for reserved time and scheduling impact.
What happens if the worker cancels? ›
If a worker cancels or does not show up, you will receive a full refund.
Can I reschedule instead of canceling? ›
Yes. Reschedule request must be approved by both the customer and worker.
Can cancellation fees be waived? ›
Yes. Taskmint may waive cancellation fees for safety concerns, fraudulent activity, or approved special circumstances. All cases will be investigated before a final decision is made.
Payments & Refunds
How do payments work? ›
Customers pay securely through the app when posting a task. Taskmint holds the payment until the task is completed. Once the customer marks the task as completed, payment is released to the worker minus Taskmints service fee.
Why is my payment being held? ›
Taskmint temporarily holds payments to help protect both customers and workers. Payments re released once the task is marked complete or automactically released after 3 days.
How do I mark a task complete? ›
Go to your Accepted Tasks page, open the task, and tap "Mark Complete" once the work has been finished to your satisfaciton.
What if I'm unhappy with the work? ›
If you're unhappy with the completed work, you can open a dispute through the task before payment is released. Our team will review the details and any submitted evidence. Please note once the payment releases a dispute cannot be opened so we recommend making sure the job was completed to your liking.
Can I request a refund? ›
Refunds may be available depending on the situation. Customers should open a dispute before payment is released so the issue can be reviewed by the Taskmint team.
Workers & Task Issues
What happens if a worker is late? ›
If a worker is running late, they should update you through the app messages. Customers may cancel tasks that remain unresponsive or excessively delayed based on Taskmints cancellation policy.
Can I report a worker? ›
Yes. Customers can report workers directly through the app if they experience inappropite behavior, safety concerns, spam, or policy violations.
Can I upload photos of my task? ›
Yes. Customers can upload photos when posting a task to help workers better understand the job details before sending quotes.
Account & Safety
Why was my account restricted or suspended? ›
Acciunts may be restricted or suspended for safety concerns, policy violtions, fraudulent activity, abusive behavior, or repeated cancellation issues.
Have all workers had a background check? ›
No. Workers are not currently required to complete a background check. Customers should review worker profiles, ratings, reviews, and verification detais before accepting a quote.
Can workers see my exact address before I accept them? ›
No. Workers only receive limited location details until you accept their quote to help protect customer privacy and safety. Your address will become visible once the task is 7 days due so that they may plan their work schedule.
Can I post task for someone else? ›
Yes. You can post a task for someone else, but you are responsible for providing accurate details and ensuring someone is available for the appointment if needed.
Can multiple workers send quotes? ›
Yes. Multiple workers can send quotes for the same task, and customers can review profiles, ratings, and pricing before choosing who to hire.
Do I have to accept the lowest quote? ›
No. Customers are free to choose the worker they feel is the best fit based on pricing, ratings, reviews, experience, and communication.
Can I reopen a cancelled task? ›
Canceled tasks cannot be reopened, but customers can create a new task at any time.
What types of task are not allowed? ›
Harrassment, threats, discrimination, hate speech, scams, spam, sexual content, and request to pay outside of Taskmint are not allowed on the platform and will result in being banned.
What items or services are not prohibited? ›
Taskmint does not allow illegal items, weapons, drugs, hazardous materials, adult services, scams, or task that violate local laws or safety policies.
How long do refunds take? ›
Refund processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider, but most approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.
What happens if there is property damage? ›
Customers should report any property damage through the dispute center as soon as possible and provide photos or supporting evidence for review.
What happens if a worker ask for off platform payment? ›
Workers are not allowed to request off-platform payments. If this happens, customers should report the conversation thorugh the app for review.
Is my payment information secure? ›
Yes. Taskmint uses secure payment processing and does not store full payment card details directly on the platform.
