How TaskMint Works

Find answers about posting tasks, accepting work, fees, payments, disputes, and other important information.

  • Tap post a task.
  • Next you'll see a menu of task that can be completed on our platform. Pick what you need done.
  • Answer the required questions.
  • Enter your address.
  • Tap post task.
  • Your task is now visible to all workers in your area who can help.
  • Find a task that you're interested in.
  • Send a short but convincing message to the customer and provide a quote for the task available.
  • Agree on amount, terms, and date for the task.
  • Within the task tap on send quote and enter your agreed amount and send it.
  • Your customer will have 3 days to pay the invoice before it expires.
  • Customers are not charged any fees. Workers contribute a 15% service fee for access to the platform, including proteccted payments, dispute support, and job opportunities.
    • We strive to remain transparent so if you have any questions about a payment please email us at workersupport@trytaskmint.com.

    Workers submit a quote for the job. Once the customer accepts the quote, payment is made immediately. Funds are securely held by our platform and are not released immediately. After the job is completed, the customer has up to 3 days to confirm completion. If no issues are reported within that time, the payment is automatically released to the worker. A 15% service fee is deducted from the worker's payout. Customers are not charged any additional fees. If a concern is raised, the funds remain on hold while the issue is reviewed and resolved.

      If a customer reports an issue within 3 days, the payment is placed on hold. Both the customer and worker MUST submit details and evidence. After review, a final decision is made and the funds are either released to the worker, partially refunded, or fully refunded to the customer. Disputes can take up to 7 business days to be completed depending on the evidence provided.

    Always review worker's profiles, ratings, and past work before accepting a quote. Make sure the job details, timeline, and total price are clearly agreed on upfront. Payment is required once you accept a quote and will be held securely until the job is completed. Communicate through the platform only and keep all agreements, updates, and expectations documented for protection.

    Review the job details, location, timeline, and exptectations carefully before submitting a quote. Make sure your quote reflects the full scope of work as pricing should be final once accepted. You are responsible for completing the job as agreed and communicating clearly with the customer through the platform. Payment is released after the job is completed and approved (or automatically after 3 days). A 15% service fee will be deducted from your payout. Ensure at your arrival to your job to always retrieve the arrival pin code so that you aren't marked as a no show. If you don't retrieve this pin then we are unable to verify you completed this task unless the customer advises you have. Failure to complete the job as agreed may result in disputes, delayed payment, or account action.

    Once you accept a workers quote, your payment is securely held by Taskmint until the task is completed. The worker will receive your task assignment and be able to contact you through the app. You will be able to:

  • Message your worker
  • Track task progress
  • Confirm arrival
  • Mark the task complete
  • Open a dispute if needed

    • For safety and verification purposes, every assigned task includes a uniqure arrival code. When the worker arrives: 1. Ask the worker to confirm they are there 2. Provide the arrival code shown inside your task details. 3. The worker enters the code in the app. 4. The task officially begins. Never provide the arrival code before the worker arrives at the location.

    Payments are securely held while the task is being completed. Once the task is finished. You'll have up to 3 days to confirm completion or report a problem. If no dispute is opened during that time, funds are released to the worker automatically. Taskmint deducts the worker platform fee before payout.

    If something goes wrong, you can open a dispute directly from the task details page. Examples include:

  • Worker never arrived
  • Property damage
  • Incomplete work
  • Safety concerns
  • Wrong service completed
    • Our team may review:
  • Messages
  • Photos
  • Task details
  • Timeline activity
  • Arrival verification

    • Task may be cancelled before a worker begins. However: Cancelling after a worker has already traveled or started work may result in fees. Frequent cancellations may affet account standing.

    For your protection, all communication should remain inside Taskmint messaging and calling feature. Keeping conversations in-app helps:

  • Protect payments

  • Support Disputes
  • Improve account security

  • Pricing

    Taskmint provides the platform connecting customers and workers. Workers are independent users of the platform. Customers should:

  • Review worker profiles carefully
  • Read ratings and reviews
  • Communicate expectations clearly before hiring

    • Payments are held to help protect both the customers and workers. This system helps:

  • Prevent non-payment
  • Reduce scams
  • Support disputes
  • Verify task completion before funds are released
