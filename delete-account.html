Deactivate your customer profile?

Deleting your account will deactivate your customer profile in TaskMint. Your profile record will stay securely stored for history, payment records, completed task records, dispute records, and legal/security purposes.

This action cannot be undone.

What happens after deletion?

  • Your customer profile will show as deactivated.
  • You will not be able to post new tasks.
  • Workers should no longer be able to send quotes to your profile.
  • Your past task, payment, dispute, and message history can remain saved.
  • You will be signed out after the account is deactivated.

Your account will not be deleted from the database. It will only be marked as deactivated.
