About Us

TaskMint is a local task marketplace that helps customers find reliable workers for everyday jobs. Customers can post a task, review interested workers, message applicants, and choose the right person for the job.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easier for people to get help when they need it while giving workers a simple way to find paid opportunities.

How TaskMint Works

Customers post jobs, workers apply, and customers can review applicants before accepting someone for the task. TaskMint is being built to support messaging, payments, scheduling, and task updates in one place.
